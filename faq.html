FAQ

Q : When i use this app, what should i do?

A : After unfollow 20 or 30 users in this app, you can open instagram app to click anywhere, for example, follow or unfollow anyone, watch a picture or video. After do it, open this app, continue to unfollow users. This action is cyclic. Of course, you can click "open news" to review news after unfollow 30 users. After few minutes, continue to unfollow users.


Q : Why i unfollow users only 10 at once?

A : Unfollow 10 users at once is to help you reduce your unfollowing speed. This action is to help your account would not be locked by instagram.


Q : when i unfollow user, the app show "unfollow fail", how to fix it?

A : You can open your instagram app, click anywhere to make sure your instagram account is ok, then open the app to unfollow again. If it not work, you can login the app again. the "logout" button is at the right top in the main page.


Q : when i open the app, it show "Failed to connection, please check your network", how to fix it?

A : First, you should make sure your network is ok. Then you can open your instagram app, click anywhere to make sure your instagram account is ok, then open the app to unfollow again. If it not work, you can login the app again. the "logout" button is at the right top in the main page.


Q : Why my instagram account was locked by instagram?

A : You unfollow too many users in a short time, instagram maybe think your account is dangerous, they will lock your account. If you want to avoid that happen, you should not unfolow users more than 100 in a short time. After unfollow 20 or 30 users in this app, you can open instagram app to click anywhere, for example, follow or unfollow anyone, watch a picture or video. After do it, open this app, continue to unfollow users. Of course, you can click "open news" to review news after unfollow 30 users. After few minutes, continue to unfollow users.
